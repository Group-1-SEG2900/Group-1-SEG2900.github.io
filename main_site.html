Features Page

Features

Main Points

  • All in one place: Easily find all event-related content and attendees' insight in one place.
  • Different perspectives: Have a look at different people in the crowd and their experiences.
  • Connect with Event Holders: Artists, players, leaders, and other event hosts can engage with their attendees directly.
  • Visibility for All: No need for a massive following to share your photos.

Sharing Perspectives at Events

  • Collective Content: Access more event-related content from others by adding them to the event folder.
  • Shared Experiences: Share your unique perspective with other attendees.
  • Interact: Like and comment. Direct Messaging (DM) is not offered at the moment.
  • Meet New People: Connect with others you noticed at the event before, during, or after the show.

Tree Structure and Folders

  • All in one folder: All content for a specific event will be put into a folder for all eventgoers (and others) to share and see.
  • Efficient Organization: Events are split into categories (music, sports, etc) and will contain sub-folders.

Personal Profiles

  • Customizable Profiles: Create your unique profile with a username and display name.
  • Fandom Display: Showcase the artists, teams, or events you enjoy on your profile.
  • Tailored Home Feed: Select at least three interests to personalize your feed with their event photos.
  • Social Media Integration: Link your other social media accounts to your profile.

Additional Features

  • Event Suggestions: Discover events based on your interactions and interests.
  • Event Locator: Find events near you.
  • Private Events: Use Insite to share folders with chosen people (weddings, family events, private conferences, CEO-employees...)
