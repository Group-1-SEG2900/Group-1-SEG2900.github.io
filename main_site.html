Features Page

Features

Main Points

  • Organized Content: Easily find all your event-related content in one place.
  • Event Memories: See all event attendees' photos in one place.
  • Enhanced Connection: Feel more connected to people.
  • Efficient Search: Quickly find content without confusion or outdated information.
  • Visibility for All: No need for a massive following to share your photos.
  • Connect with Event Holders: Artists, players, leaders, and other event hosts can engage with their attendees directly.

1: Sharing Perspectives at Events

  • Collective Content: Access more event-related content from others by adding them to the event folder.
  • Shared Experiences: Share your unique perspective with fellow attendees.
  • Interact: Like and comment. Direct Messaging (DM) is not offered at the moment.
  • Meet New People: Connect with others you noticed at the event before or during the show.
  • Build Your Network: Attract followers with your stunning event photos.

2: Tree Structure and Folders

  • All in one folder: All content for a specific event will be put into a folder for all eventgoers (and others) to share and see.
  • Efficient Organization: Best photos are easily accessible at the folder's forefront.
  • Top-Ranked Photos: Most-liked photos rise to the top for increased visibility.

3: Personal Profiles

  • Customizable Profiles: Create your unique profile with a username and display name.
  • Fandom Display: Showcase the artists, teams, or events you enjoy to find similar individuals.
  • Tailored Home Feed: Select at least three artists or teams to personalize your feed with their event photos.
  • Social Media Integration: Link your other social media accounts for more connections.

Additional Features

  • Event Suggestions: Discover events based on your interactions and interests.
  • Event Locator: Find events near you effortlessly.
